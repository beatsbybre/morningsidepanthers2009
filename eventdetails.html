The 2009 Season

In the Seniors, it was a repeat of the 2003 Premiership decider, red hot favourites Morningside took on the fast-finishing Mount Gravatt in the battle of Brisbane's South-East. Both clubs entered with that winning feel about them with neither having lost in the previous two months to September 2009. Morningside entered fresh and desperate to amend for the previous years fade out. Backed all year long by a plethora of depth across Reserve Grade and Under 18's, the club was able to achieve the rare feat of complete Senior Club Win, something unfathomable in today's football era. An effort that shows the true character and incaptulates the formidable Morningside Panther Spirit.
